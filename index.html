SPECIAL KERALA FESTIVAL TOURS

KADHAKALI

Kathakali is a traditional Indian dance-drama art form that originated in Kerala over 300 years ago. It's a complex form of theater that combines dance, music, acting, drama, devotion, costumes, and makeup to tell stories without dialogue.

THRISSUR POORAM

Thrissur Pooram is a Hindu temple festival in Thrissur, Kerala, India that celebrates the state's cultural and spiritual heritage. It takes place annually in the Malayalam month of Medam, which is April–May, on Pooram day, when the moon rises with the Pooram star.

PULI KALI

is a folk art form and festival from Kerala, India that takes place on the fourth day of the Onam harvest festival.
